About Our Academy


Pro Tennis Academy Facility

Professional Tennis Training Since 2014

Founded by Mr. Ashish, Pro Tennis Academy has been the premier destination for tennis training in Ghaziabad for over a decade. Our mission is to nurture talent, promote fitness, and develop champions both on and off the court.

Located in the heart of Kavi Nagar, our academy features world-class facilities including synthetic courts, fitness training areas, and video analysis technology to help players improve their game.
